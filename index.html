About Bibek Books

A premium digital library and publishing platform founded by Bibek Patra.

📚 Our Mission
To democratize access to knowledge by providing a platform where readers can discover, buy, and read books from independent authors, and where authors can publish and monetize their work effortlessly.

Who We Are

Bibek Books is the brainchild of Bibek Patra, a passionate entrepreneur, developer, and author from Mayurbhanj, Odisha, India. With a deep love for reading and technology, Bibek created this platform to bridge the gap between readers and independent authors.

Our platform hosts a diverse collection of books across genres — from spirituality and fiction to science, non-fiction, poetry, and business. We believe in the power of stories and ideas to transform lives.

What We Offer

  • 📖 Curated Library: Handpicked books from independent authors across multiple genres.
  • 🔓 Free & Paid Books: Enjoy free titles or purchase premium books at affordable prices.
  • ✍️ Author Platform: Independent authors can publish, manage, and sell their books directly to readers.
  • 💳 Secure Payments: Seamless UPI payments via Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm.
  • ⭐ Community Reviews: Read and share reviews to help fellow readers discover great books.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every voice can be heard, every story can be told, and every reader can find their next favorite book. Bibek Books is committed to building a vibrant ecosystem that empowers authors and delights readers.

Join us on this journey of discovery, learning, and growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an author profile? +
Click on "Publish Your" in the menu, fill in your details, and submit. You'll need a unique username and a valid UPI ID.
How much does it cost to publish? +
Publishing on Bibek Books is completely free. You only pay a small commission when you make a sale.
How do I get paid for sales? +
Payments are sent directly to your registered UPI ID. You'll receive your earnings after each successful sale.
Can I edit my book after publishing? +
Yes, you can edit your book anytime. Go to your dashboard, find your book, and click the edit button.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept UPI payments via Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm. Razorpay handles all transactions securely.
